Overview

The Dashboard provides an objective view of performance metrics of all your projects and serve as an effective foundation for further dialogue. Also your dashboard is a business intelligence tool used to display data visualizations in a way that is understood and accessible to everyone. Dashboards use raw data from these sources, spreadsheets and databases to create tables, line charts, bar charts and gauges in a central dashboard that users can look at and immediately understand the metrics they are looking for. Data dashboards simplify end of month reporting by allowing users to communicate information at any time without hours of preparation and analysing. Dashboards can automatically generate reports with its data, at anytime, anywhere. No longer do users need to gather, analyze and format data
